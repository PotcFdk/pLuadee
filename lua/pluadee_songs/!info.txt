This directory contains the pLuadee Songs.
They have been converted with a self-made C++ parser.

Songs:

 - ziphoid-megamix88.pld.lua
   :-> Ziphoid - Megamix '88
   
 - horizon.pld.lua
   :-> Estrayk - Horizon
   
 - theme_of_cia.pld.lua
   :-> ( Unknown Artist ) - Theme Of CIA
   
 - potcfdk-brakestorm.pld.lua
   :-> PotcFdk - Brakestorm     (uses samples from  'ziphoid - megamix88' )
